About Our Organization

About Us

Empowering individuals and families is at the core of everything we do at Heart of Agatha Inc. We're a Cape Coral-based non-profit organization dedicated to tackling some of the toughest challenges facing our community: poverty, homelessness, domestic violence, mental illness, and food insecurity. But we don't go it alone. We believe in the power of collaboration. We partner with local resources to create a comprehensive support network, offering essential services

Our Approach


We're deeply embedded in the Cape Coral community, partnering with local resources to ensure our services directly address the needs of our neighbors.
We don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. We provide a comprehensive network of support, including advocacy, resource referral, and personalized programs.
We equip individuals with the tools and skills they need to become self-sufficient, fostering long-term change and breaking the cycle of poverty and hardship
