About Our Organization

About Us

Heart of Agatha was established as a Non-Profit Organization by Jocelyn Joseph-Besley. Jocelyn grew up in a household where it was a common thing to see her mother, Agatha Joseph helping others all the time. Agatha believed that children and families when nurtured and taught within a supportive environment had the potential to become successors in life. Agatha's heart was very BIG! Jocelyn wants to emulate what she saw as a child growing up by initiating a heart of service to those in need within our communities. With 20+ years in the Human Service profession, Jocelyn's experience and education along with her heart to serve, birthed Heart of Agatha Inc.

Our Approach


Heart of Agatha offers a unique approach to effectively advocate for a diverse clientele, where individuals and families can access resources and programs that will support their needs. It is our effort to focus on seeking community-based alternatives for children and families that will enhance their ability to function in a healthy, satisfying, safe and respectful environment.

Join Us

We will diligently fight to eradicate domestic violence, hunger and homelessness within our communities. Heart of Agatha Inc. will provide a host of programs that will not only strengthen families but provide them with a clearer vision to a brighter future. This we do with professionalism, integrity, commitment and responsibility in achieving our mission. Please join us in making a difference.
